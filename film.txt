Breaking bad (20 января 2008)
"Breaking Bad" follows a high school chemistry teacher, Walter White, who, after being diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer, begins manufacturing and selling methamphetamines with a former student to secure his family's financial future, gradually transforming into a criminal mastermind.
